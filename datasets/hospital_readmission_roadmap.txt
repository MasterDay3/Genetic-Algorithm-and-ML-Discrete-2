About Dataset
Hospital Patient Readmission Dataset
Synthetic dataset simulating hospital patient records to predict 
30-day readmission risk using clinical and demographic features.

Dataset Info
Date range: 2021–2023
Readmission rate: ~55%
patient_id:	Unique patient identifier
admission_date:	Hospital admission date
season: Winter / Spring / Summer / Fall
age: Patient age (18–95)
gender: Male / Female
region: North / South / East / West / Central
primary_diagnosis: 11 diagnosis types
comorbidities_count: Number of additional conditions (0–10)
length_of_stay: Days in hospital (1–30)
treatment_type: Medical / Surgical / Interventional / Conservative
medications_count: Number of prescribed medications
followup_visits_last_year: Outpatient visits in past year
prev_readmissions: Previous hospital readmissions
insurance_type: Private / Medicare / Medicaid / Uninsured
discharge_disposition: Home / Home Health / Skilled Nursing / Rehab
label	Binary	0 = No readmission, 1 = Readmitted within 30 
etc..
